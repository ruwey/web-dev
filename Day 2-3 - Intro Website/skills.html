Skill: Cycling

The activity that I am probably the best at and, certainly, what I have spent to most of my spare time doing in the last year is Biking. It is the right mix of meditative and exploratory for me to really enjoy it. I also find it a great time to listen to books, podcasts, and music as well as getting to spots around the city that I would not have found otherwise.

  • American Coast to Cost
